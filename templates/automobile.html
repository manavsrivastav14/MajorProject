What are the car loan requirements?

You’ve decided to buy a new car. You’ve test driven several models, chosen a favourite, and are ready to book the dream vehicle. But before rushing to apply for a car loan, you must ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria and have the right documents in place. This will speed up the processing of the car loan application, and you will have the car in your possession sooner.

Am I eligible for a car loan

Each bank has its own criteria for car loans. They may require a minimum annual income, years of employment or company turnover before they can approve your loan. For instance, if you are a salaried person, you may need to be employed for at least two years to be eligible for a car loan. Similarly, self-employed persons in proprietorship partnership firms or public or private limited companies should have a turnover of at least Rs. 300,000 per annum to apply for a car loan. You can always check your car loan eligibility for a new car loan

Do I need a guarantor for a car loan?

Generally, you don’t need a guarantor for a car loan. However, you may need a guarantor if you don’t fulfil the eligibility criteria. For example, if your annual income is lower than the bank’s criteria, the may ask you to furnish a guarantor.
